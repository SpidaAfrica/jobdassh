About Jobdassh

Jobdassh is an international healthcare recruitment agency based in Germany. We bridge the global healthcare talent gap by ethically connecting German-speaking candidates with hospitals, care homes, and vocational institutions in Germany.

Our Mission

To provide ethically grounded, end-to-end placement services for international healthcare candidates and support German institutions in building sustainable, skilled, and diverse teams.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted international recruitment partner for German healthcare institutions — recognized for our integrity, impact, and commitment to quality workforce solutions.
